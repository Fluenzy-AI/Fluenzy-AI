## Reporting a Vulnerability

Fluenzy AI takes security vulnerabilities seriously. If you discover a security issue, vulnerability, or potential exploit within the software or infrastructure, please report it responsibly.

### Contact Information

- Security Email: admin@fluenzyai.app
- Website: https://www.fluenzyai.app/

---

## Guidelines for Responsible Disclosure

Please follow these guidelines when reporting vulnerabilities:

- Do not publicly disclose the issue before it has been resolved.
- Do not exploit the vulnerability beyond what is necessary to demonstrate its existence.
- Do not access, modify, or delete data belonging to others.
- Do not perform actions that may disrupt services or compromise user privacy.

---

## What to Include in Your Report

To help us investigate efficiently, include the following details:

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Affected components or files
- Potential impact assessment
- Screenshots, logs, or proof-of-concept code (if applicable)
- Suggested remediation (optional)

---

## Response Process

After receiving a report, the Fluenzy AI security team will:

1. Acknowledge receipt of the report
2. Investigate and validate the issue
3. Work on mitigation or remediation
4. Notify the reporter once the issue is resolved

Response times may vary depending on severity and complexity.

---

## Confidentiality

All vulnerability reports will be handled confidentially. Reporters are expected to maintain confidentiality until the issue has been officially resolved and approved for disclosure by Fluenzy AI.

---

## Security Best Practices

Authorized users and contributors should:

- Use strong authentication credentials
- Keep access tokens and secrets private
- Avoid sharing proprietary code publicly
- Report suspicious activities immediately
- Follow all confidentiality and licensing obligations
