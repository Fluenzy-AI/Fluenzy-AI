# ✅ Assessment Quick Actions - IMPLEMENTATION COMPLETE

## 🎯 What Was Built

### 1. Quick Actions (Company Portal)
**Location:** /company/portal/assessments/[id]

Three fully functional buttons:
- **📥 Export Results** - Downloads CSV with candidate data, scores, and AI interview transcripts
- **👁️ Preview Assessment** - Shows assessment questions and details in modal
- **👥 Assign to Candidates** - Assigns assessment to job applicants with email invitations

### 2. AI Interview System (Candidate Side)
**Location:** /candidate/assessment/[token]

**Features:**
- ✅ Real-time AI interviewer using Gemini 2.5 Flash with native audio
- ✅ Voice transcription (both AI questions and candidate answers)
- ✅ Live transcript display during interview
- ✅ Automatic answer evaluation and scoring
- ✅ Results saved to company portal
- ✅ Interview saved to history for PDF audit download

**How It Works:**
1. Candidate receives email with unique assessment link
2. Clicks "Begin AI Interview" → Grants microphone permission
3. AI interviewer greets and asks 5-8 relevant questions
4. Real-time transcription shows what's being said
5. Candidate answers naturally by speaking
6. After interview ends, answers are evaluated and scored
7. Results appear in company portal & candidate's "My Assessments"

### 3. My Assessments Page (Candidate)
**Location:** /train/assessments
- Shows all assigned assessments from companies
- Displays company logo, job title, due date, status
- Quick access to start pending assessments
- Added to sidebar under "Job & Career" section

### 4. History & PDF Generation
**Location:** /history
- AI Interview transcripts saved as session records
- Download Audit PDF includes:
  - Full Q&A transcript
  - AI evaluation and feedback
  - Performance scores
  - Recommendations

## 🔧 Technical Implementation

### Database Schema
**New Model: CandidateAssessmentSession**
`prisma
- sessionToken (unique access link)
- status (PENDING|INVITED|IN_PROGRESS|COMPLETED|EXPIRED)
- score, passed, timeTaken
- aiTranscript (JSON array of Q&A pairs)
- agoraChannel, agoraToken (for Voice/GD)
- Relations: Assessment, ExternalJobApplication
`

### Key APIs Created
1. **POST /api/company/assessments/[id]/assign**
   - Assigns assessment to candidates
   - Creates session tokens
   - Sends email invitations

2. **GET /api/company/assessments/[id]/export**
   - Exports results as CSV
   - Includes AI interview transcripts

3. **GET/POST /api/candidate/assessment/[token]**
   - Loads assessment for candidate
   - Starts/submits assessment
   - Handles all 5 assessment types

4. **GET /api/candidates/assessments**
   - Lists all assessments assigned to candidate

5. **POST /api/sessions**
   - Saves interview to history for PDF generation

## 🧪 Testing Instructions

### Test Flow (End-to-End)

**Step 1: Create Assessment (Company)**
1. Go to http://localhost:3000/company/portal/assessments
2. Create new AI Interview assessment
3. Set duration (e.g., 10 minutes), passing score (e.g., 70%)

**Step 2: Assign to Candidates (Company)**
1. Open assessment detail page
2. Click "Assign to Candidates"
3. Select candidates from job applications
4. Send invitations

**Step 3: Take Assessment (Candidate)**
1. Candidate receives email with link
2. Click link → Opens /candidate/assessment/[token]
3. Review instructions, click "Begin AI Interview"
4. Grant microphone permission
5. AI interviewer starts asking questions
6. Answer naturally by speaking
7. Watch real-time transcription
8. Click "End Interview" when done
9. See results (score, passed/failed)

**Step 4: View Results (Company)**
1. Go back to assessment detail page
2. See candidate result in "Assessment Results" table
3. Click "Export Results" to download CSV with transcripts
4. CSV includes: Name, Email, Score, Pass/Fail, Transcript

**Step 5: Download Audit PDF (Candidate)**
1. Candidate goes to http://localhost:3000/history
2. Sees interview session listed
3. Clicks "Download Audit PDF"
4. PDF includes full transcript, AI feedback, scores
